The Society for Social Research (SSR) was founded in the early 1920s by the Department of Sociology as a collegial research group for advanced graduate students and faculty. Today's SSR serves as the official representative of the department's graduate students, aka, graduate student council. Through SSR, students are active on committees from faculty hiring to the future of the program.
